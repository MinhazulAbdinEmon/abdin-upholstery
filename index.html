The ABDIN difference

Stripped, re-padded, re-trimmed — all by hand.

From a single worn car seat to a full living-room re-cover, ABDIN restores comfort and character with hand-cut leather, precise stitching and a finish built to outlast the original.

We don't simply slip a cover over the problem. Each piece is stripped, re-padded and re-trimmed so it looks — and feels — better than the day it left the showroom. The same hands that re-trim a luxury car seat in the morning re-cover a family sofa in the afternoon.

  • Genuine leather, Alcantara & premium vinyl sourcing
  • Colour-matched thread & OEM-pattern stitching
  • Foam re-build for support that actually lasts
